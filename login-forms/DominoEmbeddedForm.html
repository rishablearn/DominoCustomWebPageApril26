🆕 First time? Set up your authenticator app

Before you can sign in with two-factor authentication you need to link your account to an authenticator app. This takes about two minutes.

  1. Install Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or any TOTP-compatible app on your phone.
  2. Click "Open Setup Page" below. The page will display a QR code — scan it with your authenticator app.
  3. Copy your scratch codes (one-time backup codes shown during setup) and store them somewhere safe — you can use them to log in if you lose your phone.
  4. Return here and sign in normally. The 6-digit code from your authenticator app will be requested after your password.
